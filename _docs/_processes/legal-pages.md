# Legal Pages

Public legal and Google review pages live in the Rails app.

## Routes

- `/app-info` — public Google OAuth app information page.
- `/privacy-policy` — public privacy policy.
- `/terms` — public terms of use.

## Current purpose

The pages support Google OAuth branding review and give users clear
public policy links inside the app.

The copy is original TheraSaurus copy. Do not paste competitor policy
text into these pages.

## Review notes

These pages are practical starter pages for launch setup. Before public
launch, have a lawyer review the privacy, HIPAA, refund, limitation of
liability, and governing-law sections against the final business entity,
state, vendors, and product behavior.

If the app later adds client messaging, telehealth, intake forms,
analytics advertising, or HIPAA business associate agreements, update
these pages in the same change.
